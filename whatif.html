Designed With: Adobe Illustrator

What if: Created by choosing a provided theme (The Day the Moon Blinked) and creating a narritive to then base tokens on.
Tools: Circle tool, text, pen tool, gradient tool, duplication tool, mirror tool, insert image tool, and transform tool.

This project has sparked a deep hatred of Adobe Illustrator. This is the assignment I struggled with the most. From the creative aspects of collecting images for the theme to working with the program, I struggled every step of the way. I had help from my struggle buddy, who was amazing and stuck with me and walked me through most of the things I couldn't understand. But despite my struggles, I'm glad I stuck with it because now I kind of know what I'm doing in Adobe Illustrator, and it doesn't scare me anymore, which is an improvement! Needless to say, I'm glad this one's over with.
