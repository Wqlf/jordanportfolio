Designed With: Photoshop

Bad Photoshop: Created by drawing inspiration from the artist "Lucas Blalock" to create imperfect still-lives using many tools and functions in the program.

This project is the first time I have had access to Photoshop, which has proved to make things 100% easier. I didn't have any trouble or challenges at all during this project, other than a couple of creative blocks trying to figure out how to compose my images. However, taking inspiration from our artists John Blalock and Daniel Gordon, I was able to come up with some pretty fun images. My favorite one that I worked on was the one with all of the little cat figurines. His name is Kibby, by the way. Having access to the program that we used for this class and being able to take advantage of the tutorials associated with it just made everything easier, and without having the stress of seeking out information on my own, I was able to have a lot more fun and be a lot more expressive.
